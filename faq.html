What is Guroo?

Guroo is a support platform developed by Salford Business School for the benefit of prospective students. It is intended to assist students with understanding the University prospectus through the use of information visualisation.

Is Guroo free?

Yes. While some informative content is best used with an account, all content is free of charge to access.

Will Guroo have any effect on my university offer?

No. Guroo is merely intended to give students a brief insight into what they will be studying on the course.

Why does some content require an account?

Using an account allows your responses and progress with platform content to saved so you can review it in the future. An additional benefit of an account is that you are more able to interact with the Guroo community in real-time.
