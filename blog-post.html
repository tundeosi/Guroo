Undergraduate Open Day at The University of Salford

Ryan Bamford
6 days ago 2 min read
General

Choosing the right university is a big decision – and one of the best ways of finding out what's right for you is by going to Open Days and events. Discover how we can help you achieve your goals at next Open Day on Saturday 25 June. Register your interest now to hear first when booking opens.

Sample Picture University of Salford

A degree from the University of Salford is more than just a certificate – we’ll help you to graduate with confidence, ready to step into your chosen career. We form strong partnerships with businesses and organisations from all sectors, working hand-in-hand to develop courses that are relevant to today’s challenges. Our lecturers are professionals in their fields, with many actively engaging with industry to continuously update their knowledge and inform your teaching. In today’s competitive job market, it’s important to stand out. Whether you’re clear on your career path or you’re just starting to explore your options, we’ll help you identify where you want to be, then support and inspire you to get there. From industry placements to employment opportunities, and guest speakers to practical, hands-on workshops and lectures, we’ll help you develop the skills you need to succeed on your course and in your career.


There are many things that make the University of Salford unique, but our location is one of the things that really sets us apart. We're based in the vibrant and diverse city of Salford, in the heart of England’s north west – right next to our neighbour, Manchester. Our main campus, Peel Park, offers the best of both worlds: state-of-the-art learning facilities set against beautiful green space, within twenty minutes’ walk of the centre of Manchester. We’re also the only university with a campus at MediaCity. Its stunning waterfront location at Salford Quays is also home to TV, digital and creative organisations like the BBC and ITV. When lectures finish, you don’t need to look hard to find exciting culture, sport, shopping, food and nightlife experiences right on your doorstep here in Salford. And when you’re ready to explore Manchester, getting there couldn’t be easier – it’s just a short trip by foot or on public transport.
